# Phase 07.4 Plan 04: Fix _make_result() source_layer and add attribution assertion

Added `source_layer="L0"` to the `_make_result()` test fixture and a caplog-based assertion verifying the CONTEXT INJECT log emits `L0:0.90` instead of `?:0.90`.

## Objective

`MissionContextResult` has `source_layer: str` as a required key. Its absence from `_make_result()` caused `attribution_parts` in `build_planner_context_injection()` to always emit `?:0.90` in unit tests, masking whether attribution logging was actually working.
