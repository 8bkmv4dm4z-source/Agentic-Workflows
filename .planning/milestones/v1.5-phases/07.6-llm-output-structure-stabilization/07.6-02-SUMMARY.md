## Decisions Made

- parse_action_json step=0 default keeps all existing callers unchanged — zero migration cost
- WARNING log fires immediately before json.loads(candidate) — not after; ensures log even on subsequent parse failure
- prose_prefix WARNING guarded by `prefix.strip()` — avoids false positives on bare-JSON fallback (no prose, just malformed JSON)
- TypedDict-to-BaseModel: factory function signatures unchanged — callers receive Pydantic models transparently
- graph.py uses .model_dump() at append sites: stores plain dicts in RunState for LangGraph checkpoint serialization safety
- test_handoff.py migrated from unittest.TestCase to pytest classes (needed pytest.raises and caplog fixtures)
